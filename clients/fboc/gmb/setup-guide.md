## PART 1: STEP-BY-STEP GBP CREATION

### Step 1: Visit Google Business Profile
1. Go to **business.google.com**
2. Click **"Manage now"** (top right)
3. Sign in with the business Google account (or create one at accounts.google.com)

### Step 2: Add Your Business Name
1. Click **"Add your business to Google"**
2. Type: **French Blooms OC**
3. Select category: **Florist** (primary)
4. Click **Next**

### Step 3: Choose Business Type
1. Select **"Online and at a location customers can visit"** if the studio accepts walk-ins, OR
2. Select **"Online only"** or **"I deliver goods and services to my customers"** if service-area only
3. For FBOC: Select **"I deliver goods and services to my customers"** (service-area business model)
4. Click **Next**

### Step 4: Set Service Area
Add all of the following cities/areas:
- Irvine, CA
- Newport Beach, CA
- Costa Mesa, CA
- Laguna Beach, CA
- Huntington Beach, CA
- Anaheim, CA
- Orange, CA
- Tustin, CA
- Lake Forest, CA
- Mission Viejo, CA

Click **Next**

### Step 5: Add Contact Information
1. **Phone number:** (your business phone)
2. **Website URL:** (your website, e.g. frenchbloomsoc.com)
3. Click **Next**

### Step 6: Verify Your Business
Google will offer verification options:
- **Postcard by mail** (most common — arrives in 5–14 business days)
- **Phone call / text** (if eligible)
- **Email** (if eligible)
- **Video verification** (record a short video of the business)

**Recommended:** Choose phone/text or email for fastest verification. If not available, request postcard.

Once verified, you'll receive confirmation and your profile will go live.

Absolutely! Wedding florals are one of our specialties. Our arrangements range from approximately $65 for everyday and gift bouquets to $200+ for premium seasonal designs. **Q5: Do you offer corporate floral accounts for businesses?**

Yes! We offer dedicated corporate floral accounts for Orange County offices, hotels, restaurants, and businesses. **Q6: Can I order flowers online from French Blooms OC?**

Yes, you can place orders through our website or by contacting us directly via phone or Instagram DM. **Q7: Do you create baby shower floral arrangements?**

We love celebrating new arrivals! French Blooms OC creates stunning baby shower florals, including welcome tables, centerpieces, balloon-free floral backdrops, and personalized bouquets. We work with your color palette and theme to create cohesive, Instagram-worthy arrangements. Baby shower packages can be tailored to any budget — contact us to start planning.

**Q8: Are you available for events beyond weddings?**

Yes! In addition to weddings, we handle a wide range of events including baby showers, bridal showers, birthday parties, corporate galas, holiday parties, charity events, and pop-up activations. Whether you need a single statement arrangement or full event floral design, we bring the French Blooms aesthetic to every occasion. Inquire early for large events.

**Q9: How far in advance should I book wedding flowers?**

We recommend booking your wedding florals at least 6–12 months in advance, especially for spring and fall dates which fill quickly. Popular Saturdays between April–June and September–November book out early. That said, if your date is sooner, don't hesitate to reach out — we'll always let you know our availability and do our best to accommodate you.

**Q10: Do you offer flower subscription services?**

Yes! Our monthly flower subscription is one of our most popular offerings. Subscribers receive a fresh, seasonally-curated arrangement delivered to their home or office on a regular schedule. Subscriptions make a wonderful gift and are available in monthly, bi-monthly, and weekly frequencies. Custom subscription plans are available for corporate accounts. Contact us to set up your subscription today.

## PART 7: PHOTO STRATEGY

### Why Photos Matter
GBP listings with 100+ photos receive significantly more calls and direction requests. Consistent, high-quality photo uploads signal an active, legitimate business to Google's algorithm.

---

### Photo Categories to Upload

**1. Cover Photo**  
- Use the most striking, brand-defining image available
- Ideal: A lush arrangement on a light marble surface or against a whitewashed wall
- Dimensions: 1080 x 608 px minimum; 16:9 ratio preferred

**2. Profile / Logo Photo**  
- Use the French Blooms OC logo on a white or cream background
- Dimensions: 250 x 250 px minimum; square format

**3. Product Photos (arrangements)**  
- 3–5 photos per arrangement type
- Shot on neutral backgrounds (white, linen, marble) and lifestyle settings (table, windowsill, car delivery)
- Include both close-up detail shots and full arrangement shots

**4. Behind-the-Scenes / Studio Photos**  
- Jocelyn working at the design table
- Flowers being sorted, conditioned, or wrapped
- The workspace with buckets of fresh blooms
- Hands tying ribbon, trimming stems

**5. Delivery / In-Context Photos**  
- Arrangements photographed at delivery location (with permission)
- Flowers on doorsteps, reception desks, wedding venues
- Happy clients receiving their flowers (with permission and signed release)

**6. Wedding & Event Photos**  
- Full ceremony arch setups
- Tablescapes at reception venues
- Bridal party photos featuring bouquets
- Before/after installation shots

**7. Team / Owner Photos**  
- Professional photo of Jocelyn Janz
- Action shots of Jocelyn designing or consulting

**8. Seasonal / Holiday Photos**  
- Valentine's Day arrangements (upload by late January)
- Mother's Day collections (upload by late April)
- Christmas/Holiday florals (upload by late November)
- Spring collections (March–April)
