## Day 5 — Behind the Stems

**Headline:** Behind the stems — how we build an arrangement at French Blooms OC

**Body:**
Every French Blooms OC arrangement starts the same way: with the best stems we can source that week.

We shop the flower market early — before sunrise — selecting seasonal blooms at peak freshness. Ranunculus, garden roses, sweet peas, tulips, and whatever is exceptional that week.

Back in the studio, every stem is conditioned and hydrated before design begins. We don't rush.

The French aesthetic we love isn't about symmetry or formula — it's about movement, layering, and letting flowers look as though they just came from the garden. That takes time, and we give it.

Every order — whether a $95 monthly bloom box or a $5,000 wedding installation — gets the same care at the stem level.

That's the standard we hold ourselves to.

## How to post via GMB app (iPhone)
1. Open **Google Maps** → tap your profile photo → Your Business Profile

3. Tap **Add update** → **Add post**
4. Choose **What's new** (for most of these) or **Offer** for Day 7

6. Tap **Post**

**Best time to post:** Tuesday–Thursday, 9–11am Pacific.
